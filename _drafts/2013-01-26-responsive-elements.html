Responsive sites need responsive elements.

1) Images - though if they are small images, this may not be necessary.

2) Tables - Can use responsive table methods depending on type of table

3) Navigation - an accordion menu may be useful for this. Combine top and left nav elements and show top items. Could use a toggle menu or slide in menu.
