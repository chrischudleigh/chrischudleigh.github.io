Common uses:

  • client side form validation
  • enhanced tooltips
  • ajax
  • show/hide content
  • enhanced dropdown menus - animation, delay effects
  • enhanced keyboard accessibility
  • modal/dialog boxes
  • image effects, slideshows, image galleries,  lightbox
  • responding to events - click, focus, hover, drag+drop etc
  • modifying the DOM
  • tabs, accordions, and other navigation elements
