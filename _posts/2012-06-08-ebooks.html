Formats:

ibook - apple
ePub - open
folio - adobe

The ibook app requires ios ie. you cannot read iBooks on your mac.

The ibook author runs on osx but requires a fairly new mac.

ePub is open source and standards but epub3 supports more complex layouts but there are no real readers for it.

Indesign can export to ePub but it is not semantic.

Indesign can create folio files but expensive to distribute. Alot of work to make flexible layouts in Indesign.

Why not just make a book in html, css, and javascript like webpages and then group them with ajax?
